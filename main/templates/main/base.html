About Us

Story Generating Platform

We all loves to read stories weather it is short or long, and Even sometimes we did liked to jot it down, if you don't like it atleast we all have our childhood memories of writing stories. And we all know it is such drag to write for some. So here our project comes by just specifying words or even sentences the story will generated automatically in whichever genre you want. After using this it will be so much fun to write story which automatically level up the story writing part. You can generate stories in sci_fic, thriller, horror, drama, action, superhero genre with upto 300 words.
Steps to create story :

      1. Click on the Create on story button.
      2. Enter your word or sentences (It wil not work properly if you just specify the alphabets, numbers and special symbols)
      3. Select the genre.
      4. Click on the generate button
      5. And rest is the story which you have to read.
